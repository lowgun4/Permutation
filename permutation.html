Permutation Translator

Cauchy to Cyclic

Please enter a sequence of unique natural numbers seperated by commas representing the second line of a Cauchy Two-Line representation for some permutation. For Example, if you wish to translate the permutation that maps 1 to 2, 2 to 3, and 3 to 1, you would enter 2,1,3. Press Calculate to calculate the cyclic representation of your permutation.
